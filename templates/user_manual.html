5. Diagnose & Quote

Access via Actions → Diagnose & Quote on any active job.

What you can do

  • View the screen lock type and value (read-only — set at job entry)
  • View customer notes (read-only)
  • Add service items with individual prices
  • Update job status (Diagnosing → Repairing → Ready)
  • Collect advance payments (multiple times)

Service / Quote Items

1
Click + Add Service / Part to add line items.
2
Type service name — a dropdown of common services appears.
3
Enter the price for each item.
4
Total Quote is calculated automatically.
Required: Total quote must be greater than ₹0 before saving.

Advance Payments

  • Previous advance entries are shown as read-only rows with amount, method, and date.
  • Enter a new advance amount in the input field to add another payment.
  • Advance cannot exceed the total quote amount.
  • Each advance entry is timestamped and stored permanently.
The diagnosed timestamp is recorded the first time you save a quote.

6. Deliver & Invoice

Access via Actions → Deliver & Invoice when job is Ready or Repairing.

Steps to deliver

1
Enter the IMEI / Serial Number for billing (mandatory).
2
Review invoice items (auto-filled from Diagnose & Quote).
3
Add/edit items if needed. Apply discount if any.
4
Advance Paid amount is shown (read-only — already collected).
5
Select payment method: Cash / UPI / Card / Credit.
6
Enter the Happy Code shown by the customer to verify identity.
7
Click Confirm Delivery — invoice is created automatically.
8
Or click Deliver & Print to create invoice and open print page.
FRP / Flash jobs: Aadhar card number is mandatory before delivery can be confirmed.
The Happy Code must match exactly. This prevents wrong device handover.

8. Customer Management

Go to Customers from the sidebar.

  • All customers are grouped by phone number.
  • Shows total business (sum of all delivered job amounts).
  • Shows Due ₹X.XX badge in red if customer has an outstanding balance.
  • Total outstanding credit banner shown at top of job history.

Job History

Click on a customer row to expand their job history. For each job:

  • View Details — opens a full details modal on the same page
  • WhatsApp Invoice — sends invoice link to customer
  • Reminder — sends a reminder that device is ready for pickup

13. Admin Panel

Login as admin to access /admin.

Admin Can:

  • View all registered shop owners
  • Enable or disable any shop account
  • Set subscription plans (30d, 1y, 2y, 3y)
  • Delete shop accounts (removes all their data)
  • See subscription status and days remaining for each shop
Caution: Deleting a shop account permanently removes all their jobs, invoices, and customer data.
